Easy to use and beautiful categories widget for your wordpress site.

== Description ==

A very simple widget that creates beautiful clickable boxes for your sites categories. Including very smooth animations and good customisation including a colorpicker to match your sites theme.  

If you experience any problems leave a message in the support page and I will be back to you as soon as possible.

== Installation ==

**From the WordPress dashboard**  

1. Go to 'Plugins -> Add New'
2. Search for 'Simple Box Categories'
3. Activate 'Simple Box Categories' from your Plugins page.

**From WordPress.org**

1. Click the download button
2. Copy/upload the downloaded 'simple-box-categories/'' folder into your '/wp-content/plugins/' folder
3. Activate 'Simple Box Categories' from your Plugins page.

**When activated**

1. Go to your dashboard, hover the 'Appearance' button and click on 'Widgets'
2. Find 'Simple Box Categories' in your 'Available Widgets' area and drag it into a widget area.
3. Customize the widget to your liking and click save.

As simple as that the widget is now activated and is now to be found in your widget area on your live wordpress site.
